# Youtube Start At 10.0
* 	Errors, suggestions, ideas, send them to our mail:
		<elinv.elinv@gmail.com>

* 	Sunday, May 25, 2019, 6:58 p.m.

    * → It works in this protocol only:
        → `https: //www.youtube.com/watch? V =`

    * → The extension records in the `[storage.local]`,
				the URL of the YouTube video that is being viewed
				the current reproduction time and the title.
    
    * → Automatically when you see that video again in some
				moment if the registration still persists in the storage.local
				of the data: url, time and title, will be positioned
				the current view of the video at the last moment when
				Was seen.

    * → It allows us then to continue watching any video from
				where we have left it before.
    
    * → Save all the videos that we see on Youtube.
    
    * → By clicking on the icon of the extension we will obtain
				a list of the videos recorded, with their corresponding
				link to the exact moment of reproduction for
				continue watching them.

## ------------------------------------------------ -------

* Important info:
    → The video in play is saved before being closed
			the tab or at the same time you switch to another video.
    → But if we unexpectedly close Firefox, if before close
			the tab of the video, allowing another tab still open,
			this last video will not be saved in the current video position.

## ------------------------------------------------ -------
## FUNCTIONS:
## ------------------------------------------------ -------

* 	→ Delete each record individually in order to have a little more
			ordered the listing.

* 	→ It has been added as information in the title of each link of the video,
			the exact moment of the video to which it will be redirected in instants.

* 	→ The option to continue watching the video in a separate window has been added
			of the size of the tab, without affecting the continue navigating.

* 	→ Improving the communication of the extension with the user:
* 	→ It is visually informed at the moment by striking out the text of the
			link with double line, which has been deleted.
			However, this link can still be recovered, if it is done again
			click on it, before losing your sight.

* 	→ Now the extension adds a button with a red background with the text in English
			"Elinv Save State" that allows you to save the status of the video, that is,
			your current playing time, link and title.
			At the precise moment when you click on it.

* 	→ Now it works faster, and asynchronously perfect.

* 	→ Added button on the visited page of Youtube:
		→ That can open a resizable window.
		→ This window will only contain the video.

*		→ Added option to filter information presented by the extension
			Regarding the videos visited.
			This makes it possible to reduce the list when you want to continue looking
			a video and the list is extensive to search without this filtering.

* 	→ The notifications have been removed from the user because in Windows they act
			in a different way than in Linux, where the extension has been developed.

* 	→ Elevated strict_min_version from 42 to 57.

    *   → Funciona en este protocolo solamente: 
            → `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=`

    *   → La extensión graba en el `[storage.local]`,
					la URL del video de Youtube que se está viendo
					el tiempo actual de reproducción y el titulo.
    
    *   → Automáticamente al volver a ver ese video en algún 
					momento	si aun persiste el registro en el storage.local
					de los datos: url, tiempo y título, se posicionará
					la vista actual del video al último momento en que
					fué visto.
		    
    *   → Nos permite entonces seguir viendo cualquier video desde
					donde lo hemos dejado anteriormente.
    
    *   → Guarda así todos los videos que veamos en Youtube.
    
    *   → Al hacer click en el icono de la extensión obtendremos 
					un listado de los videos registrados, con su correspondiente 
					link al momento exacto de reproducción para 
					continuar viéndolos.

## ------------------------------------------------ -------

*     Info importante: 
          → El video en reproducción es guardado antes de ser cerrado 
						el tab o en el mismo momento en que se cambia a otro video.
          → Pero si cerramos imprevistamente Firefox, si antes cerrar 
						el tab del video, permitiendo que otro tab aun abierto, 
						este último video no se guardará en la posición de video actual.

*  	→ Se ha agregado la opción de seguir viendo el video en ventana separada
